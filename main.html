Footprint

A carbon footprint measures the total greenhouse gas
emissions, primarily carbon dioxide, associated with
an individual, organization, or activity. It reflects
the environmental impact of energy consumption,
transportation, and lifestyle choices. Reducing one's
carbon footprint is essential for combating climate
change and preserving the planet's health.

Handprint

A carbon handprint refers to the positive
environmental impact an individual or organization
can have by reducing carbon emissions and mitigating
climate change through sustainable practices. It
emphasizes actions like energy conservation, renewable
energy adoption, and carbon offsetting to create a
net reduction in one's carbon footprint, promoting
environmental responsibility.
